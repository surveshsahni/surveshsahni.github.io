Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

Based in Hamilton, we proudly serve businesses throughout South-Western Ontario, including Niagara Region, London, Barrie, Durham Region, and all points in between.

Do you offer emergency services in all service areas?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency repairs for HVAC and refrigeration systems throughout our entire service region. We understand that HVACR emergencies don't wait for business hours.

How often should I schedule HVAC maintenance for my business?

For commercial HVAC systems in Ontario's climate, we recommend twice a year‚Äîbefore summer and before winter‚Äîto keep your system running efficiently and extend its lifespan.

What are signs my commercial refrigeration system needs repair?

Watch for fluctuating temperatures, unusual noises or odors, frost buildup, water leaks, and higher energy bills. If you notice any of these signs in your Hamilton, Niagara, or other Ontario location, contact us immediately.

Do you service residential HVAC systems?

While our primary focus is commercial HVAC and refrigeration, we do offer select residential heating and cooling services upon request, particularly in the Hamilton and surrounding areas.

What makes Delta Technical Solutions different from other HVACR companies in Hamilton?

As a family-run business with over 20 years of experience, we take a personalized approach with every client. We're committed to honesty, integrity, and building long-term relationships with businesses throughout South-Western Ontario.

What types of commercial refrigeration equipment do you service?

We service all types of commercial refrigeration equipment including walk-in coolers and freezers, reach-in units, display cases, ice machines, prep tables, bar refrigeration, and specialized cooling systems used in restaurants, grocery stores, and warehouses.

Are you available on weekends for service calls?

Yes, we offer weekend service for both emergency and scheduled maintenance calls throughout our service regions including Hamilton, Niagara, London, and surrounding areas. We understand businesses operate 7 days a week and their HVACR systems need to as well.

How can I request service?

You can request service by filling out our online service request form, or by calling our office directly at (905) 555-1234. For emergency service, we recommend calling to ensure the fastest response time.
